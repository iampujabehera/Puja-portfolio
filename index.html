Agentic AI · Fintech · FHIR
CoverageIQ
An AI agent that fights insurance denials — built as a working product, not a pitch deck.
Agentic AI Multimodal FHIR Dialogflow CX
Benefits Agent ↗ Coverage Intelligence ↗
↓
🔴 The Problem

Prior auth and benefits coverage is broken everywhere — not just healthcare. Anywhere humans must navigate dense policy documents to make real-time decisions, they need an agent, not a dashboard. I was given this as a take-home assignment. I shipped a working agent.

🔵 What I Built

Multimodal AI agent — Dialogflow CX, Vertex AI, FHIR integration. Handles natural language coverage queries, parses denial codes (CARC), and surfaces the next action — not raw policy text.

🟡 The Hard Call

I deliberately blocked coverage confirmation until CPT codes and prior auth were verified. Most people would have shown the happy path first. Clinical accuracy gated the UX — not the other way around.

Healthcare AI · B2B2C · EMR Integration
CareLoop
Closing the gap between hospital discharge and patient recovery — before it becomes a readmission.
Risk Scoring EMR Integration B2B2C UAE / GCC
See the Story ↗
↓
🔴 The Problem

Hospitals lose sight of patients the moment they walk out the door. No visibility, no follow-through, no early warning. CareLoop was built to fix that — without replacing existing HMIS systems.

🔵 What I Built

A risk-scoring layer that identifies which patients are likely to disengage post-discharge. Sits on top of existing infrastructure. Tells care teams exactly who to call and when.

🟡 The Hard Call

Cut the MVP to 3 screens — risk queue, patient detail, escalation. Killed the analytics dashboard everyone wanted. A dashboard without workflow integration is just another tab nobody opens.

Voice AI · GPT-4o · HIPAA · Azure OpenAI
Sunoh.ai
Ambient AI scribe that listens to doctors and writes the notes — with zero tolerance for hallucination.
Voice AI GPT-4o HIPAA EMR Integration
Proprietary — no public link
↓
🔴 The Problem

Physicians spend 2+ hours daily on documentation. That's time not spent on patients. Sunoh.ai listens to clinical conversations and generates structured notes automatically — but in healthcare, one wrong word is a patient safety event.

🔵 What I Built

Azure OpenAI + GPT-4o transcription, with a hallucination mitigation layer using MediSpan and IMO compendiums. Every drug, diagnosis, and procedure cross-checked before it reaches the physician.

🟡 The Hard Call

Pharmacy module: auto-resolve demographic mismatches with a full audit trail vs flag-and-pause. Chose auto-resolve. Reduced prescription hold time significantly. Stayed HIPAA-compliant.
